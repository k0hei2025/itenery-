1 Day

Arrival & Vrindavan Darshan

20 March 2025 · Thursday
🚂

Arrive Mathura Junction · 9:30 AM

Head straight to Vrindavan hostel / dharamshala to freshen up and drop luggage. Plan to be settled before noon for morning temple darshan.

🌅 Morning Before 12:00 PM
  • 🛕 Radha Raman Temple One of the most revered ancient temples of Vrindavan · self-manifested deity
  • 🛕 Radha Vallabh Temple 400-year-old temple dedicated to Radha Vallabh · serene & less crowded in morning
☀️ Afternoon 12:00 PM – 4:00 PM
  • ⛰️ Vaishno Devi Temple Miniature replica of the Jammu shrine · popular pitstop
  • 🛕 ISKCON Vrindavan Grand temple complex · Bhagwad Gita museum · great for lunch prasad
  • 🌿 Nidhivan Sacred forest where Krishna is believed to perform Raas Leela at night · mystical atmosphere
  • 🛕 Ranganath Temple (Rangji) South Indian Vaishnava style · stunning architecture blend · largest temple of Vrindavan
  • 🌊 Yamuna Marg Evening walk along the ghats · serene Yamuna view
  • 🛍️ Shopping & Street Food Loi Bazaar for pichkaris, jewelry, tilaks · try mathura peda & kachori
🌆 Evening 4:00 PM – 8:00 PM
  • 🔱 Gopeshwar Mahadev Temple Only Shiva temple in Vrindavan · unique legend of Shiva attending Raas Leela
  • 🛕 Prem Mandir Illuminated after 7 PM — the lights make it otherworldly beautiful Must See
  • 🪈 Banke Bihari Temple Most beloved deity of Vrindavan · arrive before 8 PM · experience the aarti
🌙 After aarti at Banke Bihari, wind down with dinner at a dhaba near the temple street — Dal Baati or Chaat is a must!
2 Day

Govardhan & Barsana

21 March 2025 · Friday
🌄 Morning Early Start
  • ⛰️ Govardhan Parikrama Must Do ~21 km parikrama via e-rickshaw · circumambulation of the sacred Govardhan Hill · takes 3–4 hrs
🛺 Hire e-rickshaw from Govardhan bus stand — negotiate for full parikrama round trip. Key stops: Manasi Ganga, Mukut Mukut, Dan Ghati, Kusum Sarovar.
🌸 Midday Afternoon
  • 🛕 Kirti Mandir Birthplace temple of Radha ji's mother Kirti · base of Barsana hill
  • 🛕 Radha Rani Temple (Ladli Ji) Atop Barsana hill · breathtaking views · heart of Radharani's kingdom
  • 🚶 Barsana Parikrama Short parikrama of the four hills of Barsana · scenic & spiritually charged
  • 🌳 Ghevar Van Most Important A forest grove of immense significance near Barsana · deeply sacred spot · do not miss under any circumstance
🌇 Evening Return
  • 🛺 Return to Vrindavan Head back from Barsana · relax, freshen up, evening aarti at ghat
🍽️ Have dinner near the Vrindavan temple area. Try the rabri and lassi here — some of the best in the region.
3 Day

Mathura & Gokul

22 March 2025 · Saturday
🌅 Morning Mathura
  • 🛕 Krishna Janmabhoomi Must Do The sacred birth site of Lord Krishna · Keshav Dev Temple & Shahi Idgah complex · arrive early to avoid queues
🔐 No phones or cameras allowed inside Janmabhoomi complex. Deposit at the counter. Security check is thorough — carry only essentials.
🌿 Afternoon Gokul
  • 🏞️ Raman Reti Sacred sandy ground where Krishna played as a child · incredibly peaceful · Swaminarayan temple nearby
  • 🛕 Gokul Temples Nand Bhavan (Nanda's home), Brahmand Ghat · where Yashoda fed Krishna the universe
🛺 Gokul is ~15 km from Mathura. Auto or tempo is easiest. The town is quiet and beautiful — Raman Reti is best visited barefoot, feel the sacred sand.
🌆 Evening Wrap Up
  • 🛕 Return to Vrindavan Final evening · last aarti at Banke Bihari or Yamuna Ghat · buy souvenirs, prasad
4 Day

Departure

23 March 2025 · Sunday · Return Journey
🚂 Travel
  • 🙏 Final Darshan & Departure Morning darshan if time permits · collect prasad & mathura peda boxes · head to Mathura Junction
🛍️ Don't forget: Mathura Peda (packed boxes), Tulsi malas, Gopi Chandan, and Panchamrit packets make wonderful gifts. Pick up from shops near Banke Bihari temple lane.
